3. How We Use the Data

  • Authentication: account data used solely to identify the logged-in user.
  • Push notifications: FCM token used to send kick-off alerts, goals, and results.
  • Advertising: AdMob uses the advertising identifier to display relevant ads. Can be disabled in Android settings.
  • Analytics: aggregated and anonymized data to identify bugs and improve the app.
  • Local data: favorite teams and preferences stay on the device to personalize the experience.
⚠️ Match Time Pro does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for its own commercial purposes.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Firebase account: retained while active. Delete via Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  • FCM token: renewed periodically by the system, not linked to identifiable data.
  • Local data: deleted when the app is uninstalled.
  • AdMob and Analytics data: managed by Google per their retention policies.
